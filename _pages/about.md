    <p>I am a fourth-year PhD student in the <a href="https://dornsife.usc.edu/ling/">Department of Linguistics</a> at the University of Southern California, advised by <a href="https://sail.usc.edu/~lgoldste/me/">Louis Goldstein</a> and <a href="https://sail.usc.edu/people/shri.html">Shri Narayanan</a>. I am also a trainee in the <a href="https://sites.usc.edu/hcn/trainees/">Hearing and Communication Neuroscience</a> program and a member of <a href="https://sail.usc.edu">SAIL</a> and <a href="https://sail.usc.edu/span/">SPAN</a>.</p>

    <p>My research focuses on experimental phonetics, computational speech science, and speech neuroscience. Before USC, I completed an MA in Linguistics at <a href="https://linguistics.unc.edu/">UNC-Chapel Hill</a> advised by <a href="https://users.castle.unc.edu/~moreton/">Elliott Moreton</a> and <a href="https://chass.ncsu.edu/people/jimielke/">Jeff Mielke</a>, and I previously lectured at <a href="http://en.shisu.edu.cn/">Shanghai International Studies University</a>.</p>
